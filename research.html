Research

My research focuses on the application of AI/ML to solve real-world problems.

Topics

  • Building LLM agents for email and text communication channels

    This research focuses on developing advanced language models capable of handling various communication channels. The goal is to improve efficiency and accuracy in automated responses, potentially revolutionizing customer service and personal assistance technologies.

  • Evaluation of LLMs, with an emphasis on multilingual capabilities

    This topic involves creating comprehensive evaluation frameworks for Large Language Models, particularly assessing their performance across multiple languages. The research aims to improve the global applicability and fairness of AI language technologies.

  • Semantic segmentation in high-resolution, multiband drone imagery

    This research explores advanced image processing techniques for drone-captured imagery. By leveraging multiple spectral bands and high-resolution data, the goal is to improve object detection and classification in aerial imagery, with applications in agriculture, urban planning, and environmental monitoring.
